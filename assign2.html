🎬 F1 (2025)

Release Year: 2025
Director: Joseph Kosinski (Top Gun: Maverick)
Production Company: Apple Original Films, Warner Bros. Pictures, Jerry Bruckheimer Films

Plot Summary: The F1 movie follows a former driver (Brad Pitt) who returns to Formula 1 after a terrible crash ended his career. He mentors a young rookie named Joshua Pearce (Damson Idris) and together they join a small team called Apex GP. They try to compete against the big teams like Ferrari and Red Bull. The movie was filmed at real races including the British Grand Prix and Las Vegas Grand Prix. Real F1 champion Lewis Hamilton helped produce the movie to make everything look authentic.

📖 Source: Formula 1 Official Website

⭐ FAVORITE MOVIE: F1 (2025) ⭐

Release Year: 2025
Director: Joseph Kosinski
Production Company: Apple Studios, Jerry Bruckheimer Films

More Details: The movie used real F1 cars built specifically for filming. Cameras were mounted on helmets and inside the cars to capture the speed. The cast trained with real racing drivers. The movie was originally called "Apex" but changed to just "F1". Lewis Hamilton served as a producer and advisor to make the racing scenes realistic. Filming took place during actual Grand Prix weekends with real crowds and real race teams.

🎬 Source: IMDB F1 Movie Page
